## TL;DR
OpenAI plans to outpace its competitors by locking in users and expanding its corporate business, with a quarterly growth target of 20%. This move is driven by the need to increase competitiveness in the AI market, where companies like Anthropic are gaining popularity. The expansion of corporate business involves developing new applications that utilize language technology, similar to those described in the [Autonomous Developer](/blog/autonomous-developer-2026) model.

## What's Really Happening
OpenAI, a leader in the field of artificial intelligence, particularly with language models like ChatGPT, is finding itself under pressure from other innovative companies, such as Anthropic. This competition is prompting OpenAI to seek new strategies to consolidate its leadership in the market. An internal company memo emphasizes the need to expand corporate business, indicating that OpenAI sees significant potential in collaborating with large enterprises to develop personalized AI solutions. This approach will allow the company not only to maintain its market position but also to substantially increase its competitiveness.

## Why This Changes the Game
The expansion of corporate business and locking in users through personalized solutions changes the rules of the game in the AI market. This means that companies like OpenAI must not only be innovative in technology but also be able to work with large clients, understanding their needs and offering them unique solutions. For Solo Founders working on their own autonomous businesses according to the [Solo Founder Model](/blog/solo-founder-model-2026), this means the need for rapid adaptation to new market requirements and the development of unique offerings that meet the needs of large enterprises.

## What This Means for You
For those looking to successfully compete in the technology market, especially in AI, this means the need to develop in-depth knowledge of the needs of large enterprises and the ability to create personalized solutions. It also underscores the importance of continuous innovative development and adaptation to market changes. For those just starting their autonomous business, it's essential to note that developing unique solutions for large clients can be a key factor in success.
