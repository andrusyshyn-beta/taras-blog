## How does Mythos prove its superiority in mathematics and logic?

Mythos solves a record **97.6%** of the most difficult USAMO math problems, setting a new standard for the industry. This means the model is capable of more than just calculation—it can build complex, multi-step logical proofs that are inaccessible to most university-educated people. This capacity for reasoning is the foundation for building [autonomous business systems](/en/blog/solo-founder-model-2026).

**The Mathematical Breakthrough:**
USAMO involves problems where you must prove a theorem or construct a logical chain several pages long. Anthropic's previous best model, Opus, solved only **42.3%**. Thus, a model that couldn't solve more than half now can "hardly fail" at any. This is a fundamental shift from "probabilistic guessing" to "precise thinking."

## Can AI independently fix complex bugs in code?

On the SWE-bench test, Mythos solves **93.9%** of real-world software coding tasks on GitHub, an absolute record. The model is capable of independently finding errors among thousands of lines of code and proposing working fixes. This makes it an ideal tool for [Autonomous Development](/en/blog/autonomous-developer-2026), allowing a single specialist to maintain complex systems independently.

**The Programming Numbers:**
Where an experienced Senior Developer spends anywhere from an hour to a day finding and fixing a single bug, Mythos does it in seconds. It analyzes the entire repository context, understands dependencies, and produces code that passes tests in 94 out of 100 cases.
