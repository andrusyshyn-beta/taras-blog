## TL;DR
- Anthropic has raised $300 million in investments.
- This significantly increases the potential for artificial intelligence (AI) development within the company.
- The investments can be used to expand AI capabilities in solving complex tasks.

## What Actually Happened
Anthropic, a company specializing in the development of advanced artificial intelligence technologies, has raised a substantial amount of investments - $300 million. This financial support is a significant step forward for the company, as it provides the opportunity to expand research and development of new AI technologies. Such investments demonstrate a high level of trust in the company by investors and confirm the perspectives of the directions that Anthropic has chosen for development.

## How This Multiplies a Specialist by 100x
Raising significant investments allows the company to significantly expand its capabilities in the field of artificial intelligence. This, in turn, can be used to develop solutions like [Judgment-as-a-Service (JaaS)](/blog/judgment-as-a-service-pillar), which enable specialists to digitize their experience and knowledge, thereby increasing their productivity by hundreds of times. The use of such solutions can become a key step in implementing the [Solo Founder Model](/blog/solo-founder-model-2026), where one specialist can manage large projects that previously required a large team. Additionally, investments in AI can help in developing the role of the [Autonomous Developer](/blog/autonomous-developer-2026), where a developer can work independently, using autonomous tools for software development.

## Conclusion
Raising $300 million in investments by Anthropic opens up new opportunities for the development of artificial intelligence technologies.
