## Why do static creatives bring more sales than Reels?

A statistical paradox exists: Reels provide 4x more reach, but statics with a clear message generate 60-70% of all conversions. The reason is simple—people in Reels are in "content consumption" mode, not decision-making mode. A static image catches a person when they are already ready for action.

**What really works:**
- **Authenticity:** A customer's selfie-video for $200 consistently beats studio production for $50,000. The algorithm sees when people "skip" ads that look like ads.
- **Advantage+ Creative:** Instead of choosing formats, test everything simultaneously. Give the algorithm 10-15 variants, and it will set priorities itself. This is critical for the [Solo Founder model](/en/blog/solo-founder-model-2026).

## How to break the "learning trap" and set up the math of success?

The biggest e-commerce mistake is a budget too low, preventing the algorithm from exiting the **Learning Phase**. The system needs **50 optimization events per week**. If your conversion price is $20, then a $100/day budget provides only 35 sales per week. You stay in perpetual learning.

**The Math of Results:**
1. **Minimum Threshold:** $100/day is the technical minimum, but often it's money down the drain.
2. **Effective Start:** $200-300 per day allows the system to "digest" data in a week instead of a month and find a working segment.
3. **Conversion API (CAPI):** Pixel is only half the picture. Without CAPI, you lose up to 30-40% of the signals Advantage+ learns on. You pay twice as much for the same result because the system "doesn't see" half your sales.

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Can I run Advantage+ with a budget under $100/day?**  
It's possible, but risky. You'll collect 50 events too slowly, and every creative change will reset the learning. Better to aggregate the budget and run an intensive 2-week test.

**Why Conversion API if Pixel works?**  
Pixel is blocked by browsers and iOS. CAPI transmits data directly from the server, restoring up to 40% of lost signals. This is fuel for your ROAS.

**Why are statics better than video?**  
Statics communicate a specific offer better. Video is about lifestyle and reach. For fast sales with clear ROI, statics remain a staple tool in 2026.
