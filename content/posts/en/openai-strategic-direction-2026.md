## TL;DR
OpenAI presents a new report showing that over 100 million individuals used ChatGPT monthly, with over 70% being active users. The report also highlights the growth of corporate business, with over 50 enterprises using OpenAI technology for process automation. Additionally, the report points to the need to retain users by improving the ChatGPT interface and features.

## What's Really Happening
The OpenAI report testifies to the massive adoption of their technology, especially ChatGPT, among a broad audience. This fact speaks to the significant interest in artificial intelligence capabilities and the desire for people to have access to tools that can simplify their lives and work. The breakdown of users also shows that the younger generation of users is the most active in using these technologies, indicating future growth in demand for similar tools. Furthermore, the report emphasizes the need to retain users by continuously improving the interface and features of their products, which is a key aspect for any company operating in the technology sector.

## Why This Changes the Game
The OpenAI report indicates that companies developing artificial intelligence must now compete not only for the number of new users but also to retain existing ones. For the [Solo Founder Model](/blog/solo-founder-model-2026), this means paying even more attention to working with existing clients and refining products according to their needs. Such an approach will not only increase client loyalty but also help the company grow through positive recommendations and repeat purchases. Additionally, the growth of corporate business in this area emphasizes the opportunity for [Autonomous Developer](/blog/autonomous-developer-2026) to engage in developing solutions for businesses using artificial intelligence technologies.

## What This Means for You
For those interested in developing their own artificial intelligence-based businesses, this report should serve as a call to action.
