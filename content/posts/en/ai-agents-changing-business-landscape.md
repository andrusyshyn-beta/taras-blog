# How AI Agents Are Changing the Business Landscape in 2026
## TL;DR: The Main Point in 30 Seconds
*   AI agents are transforming traditional business models
*   Integration of AI into business strategies
*   New opportunities for entrepreneurs and investors
## The Scene for Change
In 2026, the world of business is undergoing revolutionary changes under the influence of technological innovations. AI agents, in particular, are playing a crucial role in this process, as they are capable of automating and optimizing various business processes.
## In-Depth Analysis
According to recent studies, by the end of 2027, over 70% of the world's enterprises will be using AI agents to improve their operations. This means that companies that do not utilize the potential of AI risk losing their competitiveness in the market.
One of the key areas of application for AI agents is the optimization of supply chains. With the ability to analyze large amounts of data and predict future trends, AI agents can help companies reduce costs and increase the efficiency of their operations.
Another important area is the use of AI agents in customer service. AI agents can efficiently process a large volume of customer requests, thereby providing high-quality service and increasing customer satisfaction.
## Verdict: What to Do Now
Entrepreneurs and investors should carefully consider the possibility of integrating AI agents into their business strategies. This will allow them not only to increase the efficiency of their operations but also to take a leading position in the market.
