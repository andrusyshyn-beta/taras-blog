## How does Managed Bots change the architecture of autonomous business?

Managed Bots is a Telegram technology that enables a "parent" orchestrator bot to automatically register, configure, and coordinate a network of highly specialized "child" bots. This transforms the messenger from a simple communication tool into a full-scale platform for deploying and managing complex agentic ecosystems without the need for manual integration of each component.

This shift from single bots to hierarchical systems perfectly complements my [Solo Founder Model](/en/blog/solo-founder-model-2026), where one individual oversees an entire staff of digital employees.

## What are the real-world business use cases for Managed Bots?

Managed Bots allow SaaS platforms to automatically provide each new client with a personalized bot featuring their unique settings directly upon registration. Agencies can deploy individual brand bots for dozens of clients from a single management panel, and educational projects can create a personal assistant bot for every student.

This effectively removes the developer as a bottleneck in the scaling process. Once configured, an orchestrator bot is capable of spawning thousands of working copies for specific tasks.

## ## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

### What are Managed Bots in Telegram?
Managed Bots is a feature that allows a bot to act as an administrator or creator of other bots. It automates the process of creating, configuring, and managing large-scale bot networks without manual intervention through BotFather for each individual instance.

### What is the difference between a regular bot and a Managed Bot?
A regular bot has fixed logic for a specific task. A Managed Bot is an orchestrator that can dynamically deploy new bots for specific clients, tasks, or projects, providing scalability that was previously impossible.

### Who benefits most from using Managed Bots right now?
SaaS products, marketing agencies, and solo entrepreneurs stand to gain the most. It enables the creation of personalized services for every user (e.g., providing each client with their own AI manager in a split second).
